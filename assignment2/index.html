Kenshi Yonezu

Kenshi Yonezu (米津 玄師, Yonezu Kenshi) is a Japanese musician, singer-songwriter, record producer, videographer and illustrator. He began releasing Vocaloid music under the stage name Hachi (ハチ) in 2009.

YOASOBI

Yoasobi is a Japanese pop duo formed in 2019 by Sony Music Entertainment Japan. It is composed of musician and record producer Ayase and singer-songwriter Lilas Ikuta, under the moniker Ikura.

LISA

Risa Oribe (織部 里沙, Oribe Risa, born June 24, 1987), known professionally as Lisa, is a Japanese singer and songwriter from Seki, Gifu, signed to Sacra Music under Sony Music Artists.
